1. Go to the calendar card in your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com).
2. Click the settings button in the top right.
3. Click "Connect Google Calendar", "Connect Apple Calendar", or "Connect Outlook Calendar".
4. If signing in with Google or Outlook, you will be redirected to the respective service to sign in.
5. If signing in with Apple, you will have to generate and enter an [app-specific password](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT211819).
6. Once connected, you can tell Martin to add an event to your calendar, and he will add it at the specified time with the specified details.

## iOS app

1. Tap the account icon in the [Martin app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1).
2. Under "Integrations", select "Calendar".
3. Click "Add Google Calendar", "Add Apple Calendar", or "Add Outlook Calendar".
4. If signing in with Google or Outlook, you will be redirected to the respective service to sign in.
5. If signing in with Apple, you will have to generate and enter an [app-specific password](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT211819).

## Example Commands
- "Add every event from this email to my calendar with detailed descriptions."
- "Add the list of events on this PDF to my calendar including locations."
- "Schedule a meeting with dawson@trymartin.com at 11am with a Google Meet link."
- "Reschedule my meeting with Shawn tomorrow at 2pm to 3pm."
- "Block off Friday from 3pm to 5pm because I'm on a plane."

## Background Tasks

In your calendar Settings in the [Martin web app](https://app.trymartin.com), you can configure Martin to call you every morning and brief you on your schedule for the day. This feature is currently only available to Pro users.
