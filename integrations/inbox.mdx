1. Go to the inbox card in your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com).
2. Click the settings button in the top right.
3. Click "Connect Gmail" or "Connect Outlook".
4. You will be redirected to the respective service to sign in.
5. Once connected, you can tell Martin to read, draft, and reply to emails on your behalf.

## iOS app

1. Tap the account icon in the [Martin app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1).
2. Under "Integrations", select "Inbox".
3. Click "Connect Gmail" or "Connect Outlook".
4. You will be redirected to the respective service to sign in.

## Example Commands
- "Check for new emails from dawson@trymartin.com today."
- "Draft a reply to Dawson's new email saying I can meet tomorrow at 9am."
- "Read me the email about the project plan out loud."
- "Forward the project plan to ethan@trymartin.com and ask for his input."
- "Email Eric the Zoom link for the meeting today on my calendar."

## Background Tasks

In your inbox Settings in the [Martin web app](https://app.trymartin.com), you can configure Martin to send you a daily digest of your emails. This feature is currently only available to Pro users.
