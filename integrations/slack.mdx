1. Go to your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com).
2. Click the integration settings button at the top of the left side bar.
3. Click "Slack".
4. Click "Connect Workspace".
5. You will be redirected to Slack to sign in.
6. Once connected, you can tell Martin to read, send, and reply to Slack messages on your behalf.

## iOS app

1. Tap the account icon in the [Martin app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1).
2. Under "Integrations", select "Slack".
3. Tap "Add Slack Workspace" and sign in with your Slack account.

## Integrations

Martin syncs with your Slack accounts and workspaces.

## Example Commands
- "Check for new messages in #general."
- "Reply to #random with 'I'm on it.'"
- "Send @Ethan a message with the Zoom link for the meeting today."
- "React to @Ethan's message in #random with a thumbs up."
- "Pin the message I just sent in #general."
