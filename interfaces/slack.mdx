## Setup (web)

1. Go to your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com).
2. Click the integration settings button at the top of the left side bar.
3. Click "Slack".
4. Click "Connect Workspace".
5. You will be redirected to Slack to sign in.

## Setup (iOS app)

1. Tap the account icon in the [Martin app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1).
2. Under "Integrations", select "Slack".
3. Tap "Add Slack Workspace" and sign in with your Slack account.

<Tip>
    Separately, Martin can send and react to messages from your own Slack account on command.
</Tip>
