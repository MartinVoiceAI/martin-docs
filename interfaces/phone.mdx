<Tip>
    Have no Wifi but need to look something up or check your email? Give Martin a call!
</Tip>

## Setup

1. Tap the account icon in the app and select "Phone Call".
2. Tap "Add your phone number" and enter your phone number.
3. Verify your phone number by entering the code you receive via SMS.
