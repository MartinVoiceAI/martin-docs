## Setup (web)

1. Go to your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com).
2. Click the account icon at the bottom of the side bar.
3. Click "Add a primary email".
4. Enter your email address in the email field.
5. Verify your email address by entering the code you receive via email.

## Setup (iOS app)

1. Tap the account icon in the [Martin app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1).
2. Under "Contact", tap "Email".
3. Enter your email address.
4. Verify your email address by entering the code you receive via email.

<Tip>
    Got an email with events coming up? Forward it to Martin, and he'll add them all to your calendar.
</Tip>
