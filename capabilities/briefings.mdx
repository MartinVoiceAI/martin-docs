## Example Briefing Topics

- My schedule for today
- Sports and Finance news
- Important emails from my team
- My to-do list
- Weather updates


## Setup

1. Tap the hammer icon in the Martin app and select "Briefings".
2. Select the frequency, topics, and send time of your briefing.
