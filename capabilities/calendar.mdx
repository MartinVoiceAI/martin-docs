Martin syncs with your existing calendar accounts.

<Card title="Apple Calendar" icon="calendar" horizontal="true">

## Example Commands
- "Add every event from this email to my calendar with detailed descriptions."
- "Add the list of events on this PDF to my calendar including locations."
- "Schedule a meeting with dawson@trymartin.com at 11am with a Google Meet link."
- "Reschedule my meeting with Shawn tomorrow at 2pm to 3pm."
- "Block off Friday from 3pm to 5pm because I'm on a plane."


## Setup

1. Tap the account icon in the Martin app and select "Calendar".
2. Tap "Add Google Calendar" or "Add Apple Calendar".
3. If signing in with Google, you will be redirected to Google to sign in.
4. If signing in with Apple, you will have to generate and enter an [app-specific password](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT211819).
