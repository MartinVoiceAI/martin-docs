1. Go to the reminders card in your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com).
2. Click the settings button in the top right.
3. Select which interfaces you want to use for reminders (App Notifications, SMS, Phone Call, Email, or Slack).
4. Tell Martin to remind you to do something, and he will ping you at the specified time, via the specified interface.

## iOS app

1. Tap the account icon in the [Martin app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1).
2. Under "Tools", select "Reminders".
3. Select which interfaces you want to use for reminders (App Notifications, SMS, Phone Call, Email, or Slack).

## Example Commands
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SMS Reminders">
    - "Text me the weather every morning at 9am."
    - "Remind me to water the plants on Monday and Wednesday."
    - "Text me to pack my luggage at 10pm."
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Phone Call Reminders">
    - "Call me every Sunday this semester to plan my meals for the week."
    - "Give me a wake up call every morning at 8am with a joke."
    - "Call me 10 minutes after my meeting with Todd to debrief." 
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Reminders">
    - "Send me an email reminder on the first of every month to review finances."
    - "Email me to leave the office at 5:30pm daily for the next 2 weeks."
    - "Email me to take a break in 40 minutes."
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Slack Reminders">
    - "Remind me on Slack to review the project plan at 2pm."
    - "DM me on Slack to check our metrics at 10am every Monday."
    - "Ping me on Slack periodically to finish the report by Friday."
  </Tab>
