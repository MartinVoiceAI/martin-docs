Martin will do some research and create an agenda for the meeting ahead of time.

## Example Meeting Agendas

- Work plans for the next 2 weeks
- Travel plans for October
- Study session for the next exam
- Diet and exercise plan for the next week
- Mental health and productivity check-in

## Setup

1. Tap the hammer icon in the Martin app and select "Meetings".
2. Optionally turn on daily morning syncs and evening debriefs.
3. Manage your upcoming meetings with Martin.
