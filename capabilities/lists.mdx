## Example Commands

- "Add each of my starred emails from today to my customer list."
- "Add the meetings from my calendar today to my to-do list."
- "Mark all my work-related todos as complete."
- "Add 'Saving Private Ryan' to my watchlist."
- "Add 'Eric' and 'Andrew' to my invite list."

## Managing lists 

- In your [Martin dashboard](https://app.trymartin.com), you can create, view, and manage your lists in the "Lists" card.
- In the [iOS app](https://trymartin.com/#section-1), tap the account icon and under "Tools", tap "Lists" to manage your lists.
